pywrdrb.parameters.VolBalanceNYCDownstreamMRF_step2Nev#

class pywrdrb.parameters.VolBalanceNYCDownstreamMRF_step2Nev(model, predicted_nonnyc_gage_flow_delMontague_lag1, mrf_target_delMontague, predicted_nonnyc_gage_flow_delTrenton_lag3, max_flow_delivery_nj, mrf_target_delTrenton, mrf_target_individual_neversink, flood_release_neversink, max_release_neversink, prev_release_reservoirs, **kwargs)#

Calculates the total releases from Neversink needed to meet the Montague and Trenton flow targets, after subtracting out predicted flows from the rest of the basin plus Cannonsville & Pepacton releases from day before and adding max deliveries to NJ, and subtracting mandated individual FFMP releases for Neversink
